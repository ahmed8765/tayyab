About Star brand

Star Organic Farm was formed in the year 1988. Initially, the farm's main activities included production and trade of agro items. However, upon establishing goodwill and capturing substantial market share,it further extended it product line to secondary derivatives like fruit pulp, cooking oils and wheat flours.
